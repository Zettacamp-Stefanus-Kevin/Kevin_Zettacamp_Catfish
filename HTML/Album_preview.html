Vinyl LP pressing. 2019 release. Tim is the posthumous third studio album by Swedish producer Tim Bergling, known by the stage name Avicii. It is his first album release following his death on 20 April 2018. It includes the single "SOS". All profits from sales of the album will go towards the Tim Bergling Foundation, set up following Avicii's suicide, for mental health awareness.
